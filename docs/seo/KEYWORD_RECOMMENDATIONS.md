# Giza Article Keyword Recommendations

Research date: 2026-07-14

Market: United States, English

Source: DataForSEO Labs and Google Ads APIs. The complete responses and normalized
tables are archived in [`dataforseo/2026-07-14`](dataforseo/2026-07-14/).

## Recommended Target

- Primary keyphrase: `pyramid construction theory`
- SEO title: `Pyramid Construction Theory: Printable Giza STL Model`
- Meta description: `Explore a printable pyramid construction theory through layered Giza STL files, internal chambers, switchback ramps, and an animated build demo.`

This target is more attractive than `Great Pyramid construction theory`: it has
nearly three times the measured search volume and lower organic difficulty while
remaining an exact description of the article. The printable and STL language in
the title differentiates the page from general history summaries.

pyramids were built.

## On-Page Notes

1. Keep the editorial headline. The separate SEO title can carry the exact target.
2. Use `how were the pyramids built` naturally in a question or early paragraph,
   not as a claim that this model is the accepted answer.
3. Keep `pyramid ramp theory` near the explanation of temporary switchback ramps.
4. Use `3D printable pyramid` and `pyramid STL` near actual files and print guidance.
5. Preserve the clear speculative framing around attribution and historical proof.
